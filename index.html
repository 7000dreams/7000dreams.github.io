Welcome!

This website is a personal website which is a repository of some of my writings, reflections and activities.

Long-form reflective essays (and also, sometimes, shorter thoughts and musings playing in my mind) are found on the Reflections page. The Academics page will contain links to my academic writing and other academic materials. The Mountains page contains my travel-related writings and photo-journal. Most of the entries are based on my mountain journeys.

On the footer of each page, you will find some quotes which resonate with me, or which give me food for thought. Refresh the page for a new quote!
