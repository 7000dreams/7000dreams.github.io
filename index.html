Welcome!

This website is a personal website which is a repository of some of my writings, reflections and activities.

Long-form reflective essays (and also, sometimes, shorter thoughts playing in my mind) are found on the Reflections page. The Academics page will contain links to my academic writing and other academic materials. The Climbing page has trip notes for various hikes and climbs. The Curiousities page is mainly dedicated towards acknowledging that I learn a lot from parenting. This has many forms, but to give an illustration of what this page is geared towards: my daughter often asks something which she is curious about ("If light can travel through a vacuum, why can't sound?"), and that makes me search for the best way to explain something. I thought a collection of question and answers such as these may be of some interest to children and to parents of young children more generally as well.

On the footer of each page, you will find some quotes which resonate with me, or which give me food for thought. Refresh the page for a new quote! I hope you relish them too.
